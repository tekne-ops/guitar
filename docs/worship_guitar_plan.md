# 🎸 Worship Guitar Fingerpicking Comeback Plan

## 🎯 Objective
Become confident leading praise in church using fingerstyle guitar, with a strong repertoire of arpeggios and songs.

---

# ⏱️ Structured Practice Plan (25–30 min, 5 days/week)

## 🔹 A. Warm-up (5 min)
Play:
- C → G → Am → F

Pattern:
```
P – i – m – a – m – i
```
Focus:
- Clean sound
- Even timing
- Relaxed hand

---

## 🔹 B. Core Skill Block (10 min)
Work on 2 patterns per week.

Chord rotations:
- Week 1: G – D – Em – C
- Week 2: C – G – Am – F
- Week 3: D – A – Bm – G

---

## 🔹 C. Worship Application (10–15 min)
- Practice 1 hymn + 1 contemporary song
- Use at least 2 patterns

---

## 🔹 D. Creative Time (Optional 3–5 min)
- Improvise freely over chord loops

---

# 🎸 Fingerpicking Patterns Arsenal

## ✅ Pattern 1: Basic Worship Flow
```
P – i – m – a – m – i
```
Use: slow worship, prayer

---

## ✅ Pattern 2: Alternating Bass
```
P – i – P – m – P – a
```
Use: hymns, fuller sound

---

## ✅ Pattern 3: Rolling Pattern
```
P – i – m – i – m – a
```
Use: contemporary songs

---

## ✅ Pattern 4: Minimalist
```
P – m – i – m
```
Use: soft, emotional parts

---

## ✅ Pattern 5: 3/4 Waltz
```
P – i – m | P – i – m
```
Use: hymns (e.g., Amazing Grace)

# 🔥 Growth Strategy

## Weekly Progression:
- Weeks 1–2: Control & accuracy
- Weeks 3–4: Dynamics
- Weeks 5+: Add singing

---

## ❌ Avoid
- Mechanical repetition without feeling

## ✅ Focus
- Expression and worship intention

---

# 🙌 Final Note
Fingerpicking is ideal for intimate and expressive worship leading.
Focus on connecting patterns with the spiritual moment, not just technique.
